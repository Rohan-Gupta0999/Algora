Step 01 — Origin

Cash with
the Bank

Parliament approves the budget. Real rupees are locked in the bank vault. Every rupee is accounted for before a single token is minted.

Step 02 — Conversion

Token
Issuance

Algora mints ALGR tokens — 1 ALGR = ₹1. Every token represents real money in the vault. The supply is public, auditable, tamper-proof.

Step 03 — On-Chain

Multisig
Approval

No single minister can move funds. A collective wallet requires majority signatures. Every approval is recorded permanently on Algorand.

Step 04 — Redemption

Detokenise
& Settle

Contractors burn tokens to claim real INR from the vault. The burn is verified on-chain before any payment is released.

Step 05 — Destination

Cash with
the Human

Real INR reaches the contractor's bank account. The entire journey — from Parliament to payment — is publicly visible on the Algora ledger.
