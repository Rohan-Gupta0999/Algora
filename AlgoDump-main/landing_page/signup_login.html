Step 01 — Origin

Cash with
the Bank

Government funds originate in the Union Budget and flow through the Reserve Bank into ministry accounts — opaque, paper-trail dependent, and prone to leakage at every transfer.

Step 02 — Conversion

ALGR Token
Exchange

Every rupee of allocated funds is converted 1:1 into ALGR tokens on the Algorand blockchain. The master wallet holds equivalent ALGO collateral, creating a transparent reserve audit trail.

Step 03 — On-Chain

Token
Issuance

ALGR tokens are issued to government official wallets via multisig accounts. Every transfer requires M-of-N approvals — no single official can move funds unilaterally.

Step 04 — Redemption

Detokenise
& Settle

Once a contractor completes a verified milestone, ALGR tokens are burned on-chain and the equivalent rupee value is released — creating an immutable proof of legitimate payment.

Step 05 — Destination

Cash with
the Human

Every citizen can see every transaction in real time. Every rupee tracked from budget allocation to final payment — permanently recorded on Algorand, impossible to edit or delete.
